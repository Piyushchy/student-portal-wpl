Attendance Policy

University attendance requirements

Students are expected to attend all scheduled classes and laboratory sessions. Attendance is taken at the beginning of each class.

Absence Policy:

  • Students are allowed a maximum of 3 absences per course per semester.
  • Exceeding this limit may result in grade penalties or course failure.
  • Medical absences require documentation within 7 days of return.

Tardiness:

  • Arriving more than 10 minutes late is considered "late".
  • Three "late" marks will count as one absence.

Important Notice

Students with attendance below 75% will not be eligible for final examinations.
